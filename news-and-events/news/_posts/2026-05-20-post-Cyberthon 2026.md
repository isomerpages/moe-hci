<p>On 2 May, over 520 students from 18 schools across Singapore pitted their
infocomm expertise against a series of cybersecurity and AI challenges
online. On 9 May, contestants gathered at Hwa Chong Institution for the
Cyberthon Fiesta — a celebration packed with 11 activity booths, the 2nd
Edition of the on-site Cyber Slay Challenge, Tech Trivia, a mass trivia
session, and carnival food, whilst awaiting the release of the competition
results. Three training sessions were also hosted prior in April to build
foundational knowledge.</p>

<p>Cyberthon is an annual collaboration between Hwa Chong Institution, the
Centre for Strategic Infocomm Technologies (CSIT) and the Digital and Intelligence
Service (DIS), and this year marks the eighth iteration! It was an honour
to have Ms Jasmin Lau, Minister of State (Digital Development &amp; Information,
Education), grace the Fiesta as our Guest-of-Honour, where she commended
participants for their passion, problem-solving skills, and strategic thinking
in tackling cybersecurity and AI challenges, while underscoring the importance
of human interaction and social skills in today's digital age. Awards were
presented alongside Mr Darren Teo, Chief Executive of CSIT, and our principal,
Mr Lee Peck Ping.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

<p>Understanding (MOU) between CSIT and HCI, witnessed by MOS Lau. This partnership
is a major milestone in advancing cybersecurity and AI education in Singapore,
bringing an upgraded lab to HCI, new opportunities for educators to deepen
their AI expertise, and internship placements with CSIT for students seeking
real-world exposure.</p>

<p>Congratulations to HCI's Team "CSIT #1 fans" — <strong>Zhao Jianzhi, Cao Xizhen, Soong Yuan Jie Julian, and Jerome Lim Feng</strong> —
for clinching 1st place overall and the Artificial Intelligence Challenge
title in this year's Cyberthon! Your curiosity and perseverance are a true
inspiration to all.</p>
