<p>A historic and ambitious collaboration between Weyoung and Studio Ardent
has culminated in two music video projects featuring originally written,
recorded, and produced Chinese songs.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/College/SA1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Over nine months, students from Weyoung self-learnt the complexities of
music arrangement, studio recording, mixing, and mastering through YouTube.</p>
<p>While the songs were being mixed, Studio Ardent filmed the music videos
over 10 days around Singapore, and also learnt to edit using Adobe Premiere
Pro and After Effects.</p>
<p>《風語》is an R&amp;B-styled ballad which won the Audience’s Choice Award
at <em>A Song For You</em> Chinese Songwriting Competition 2024, hosted by
the HCI and NYGH Mother Tongue Department and Weyoung.&nbsp;</p>
<p>The dreamy, shoegaze track《人生的浪潮終究冲散我們》won the Merit Award at the same
competition.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Both songs touch on different aspects of youth—one on romance and the
other on treasured friendships against the passage of time. In their own
flavours, they capture the common experiences of teenage angst and bittersweetness,
while paying homage to Hwa Chong’s homegrown Xinyao roots, now carried
on and evolved by yearly batches of talented Weyoung songwriters and musicians.</p>
<p>The music videos for 《風語》and 《人生的浪潮終究冲散我們》are now available on HCWeyoung’s
YouTube channel, as well as on major streaming platforms such as Spotify
and Apple Music.</p>
