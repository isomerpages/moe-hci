<p>On 9 May 2025, over 350 talented students from various Performing Arts
CCAs came together at the NUS University Cultural Centre’s Ho Bee Auditorium
for the highly anticipated Arts Buzz Concert.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="margin-left:0px;margin-top:0px;" height="417" width="624" src="https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeojLhPa-LyvAOCvF8dPzYmsI1GnJDSnd60l4iy2EYwv7-Hc0eme4fx2bM0cYF8Mf30kav4VDD4ia6SAUAtYtVGG6FEQ_1cb8DH0EV7Lx3D9F2a4ZWMTWuvucaQtD3-EEEmNpy9Gg?key=suLx99HnkwDDJ64IaEUxMr1l">

<p>An audience of more than 1,400 guests was treated to an unforgettable
evening, featuring a diverse showcase of artistic talent —&nbsp; from the
harmonious melodies of the Chinese Orchestra and Guitar Ensemble to the
expressive performances of the Strings Ensemble and the powerful vocals
of the Choir, the concert showcased a rich tapestry of talent, concluding
with dynamic performances by the Symphonic Band and captivating dances
by the three Music and Dance (MAD) divisions.</p>
