<p>On 1st August, Hwa Chong Institution’s High School Chinese Department
and Music Department, together with the WeYoung Songwriting Club and High
School Choir, jointly organised <em>A Song For You 2025</em>, a Chinese
songwriting and singing competition.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/College/Song01.png">
</div>
<p>The annual A Song For You competition celebrates musical creativity and
performance, featuring categories in Songwriting and Singing. Participants
showcase their original works and talents live on stage, bringing together
heartfelt music and youthful passion.</p>
<p>Participants from Hwa Chong’s High School and College sections, as well
as Nanyang Girls’ High School, showcased their original works and artistic
voices on stage.</p>

<p>With heartfelt lyrics and moving melodies, the performers touched the
hearts of both the audience and the judges, presenting a musical showcase
brimming with talent.</p>
<p>Their performances reflected their passion for music and the depth of
emotion behind their art. We hope that such platforms will continue to
inspire more students to express themselves through music and other art
forms.</p>
