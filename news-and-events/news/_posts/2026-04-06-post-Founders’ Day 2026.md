<p>The school community gathered to commemorate Founders’ Day with a series
of purposeful activities.</p>
<p>As part of our school’s commitment to giving back, all C1 and C2 classes
embodied the value of 饮水思源 by assembling care packs filled with essential
household items. These were distributed to beneficiaries in need through
our partner, the Chinese Development Assistance Council (CDAC).</p>

<p>Our C1 students also began the day with a spring cleaning session, working
hand in hand to care for their shared spaces. This act reflected the very
spirit of community that Founders’ Day celebrates.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/Founder days 2026/Fo05.jpg">
</div>
<p>Building on this spirit, our interim CT representatives also took the
lead in guiding their classes through a lesson package, deepening conversations
around community needs and how we can play a part in addressing them.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/Founder days 2026/Fo06.jpg">

<p>They further deepened their understanding of service through a workshop
conducted by CDAC, where they learnt more about the needs of beneficiaries.
Students also explored the Learning Festival booths set up around the school,
which showcased diverse ground-up initiatives by our seniors and highlighted
the importance of empathy and community care.</p>

<p>In the High School section, our students stepped out to serve the community
through diverse VIA initiatives.&nbsp;</p>
<p>S1 and selected S4 classes partnered <em>Reduce @ Northwest</em> to promote
sustainable living, while S2 students distributed “Brighten Your Day” packs
to spread kindness. S3 students engaged with the elderly in community homes
and in school, and nine S4 classes worked with SATA CommHealth to teach
seniors how to monitor their blood pressure.</p>

distributing Founders’ Day Food Drive contributions to residents in need
