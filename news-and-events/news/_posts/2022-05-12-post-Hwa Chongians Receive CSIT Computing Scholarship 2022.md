<p>Eight students from Hwa Chong Institution received the CSIT Computing
Scholarship in 2022. The CSIT Computing Scholarship aims to encourage and
nurture talents in pursuing an education and future career in the tech
industry.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Students receive an annual $1000 allowance and up to $1000 claimable learning
allowance for online courses. Students attend a one-week experiential learning
at CSIT at the end of JC1 to learn hands-on cyber security skills and interact
with CSIT's engineers. The scholarship is bond-free.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Since its inception in 2021, Hwa Chong Institution has been the school
with the largest number of scholars for the scholarship.</p>
