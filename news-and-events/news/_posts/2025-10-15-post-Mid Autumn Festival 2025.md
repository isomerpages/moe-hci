<p>On 4 October 2025, Hwa Chong’s highly anticipated Mid-Autumn Festival
lit up the campus once again.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/College/MA1.png">
</div>
<p>This year’s celebration featured vibrant booths by HCAstro and the Calligraphy
Club, alongside engaging games such as 射壶 (Pitch-pot) and 许愿烁一烁 (Make a
wish), which added a playful touch to the festivities.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/College/MA2.png">
</div>
<p></p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/College/MA3.png">
</div>
<p>Beyond the booths, the annual MAF concert “烁” (Sparkle) dazzled the audience
with an impressive line-up of performances. The evening began with a captivating
piece by the Chinese Orchestra, followed by performances from the String
Ensemble, Weyoung, Chinese Dance, and Wushu. It was a heartwarming evening
that brought together alumni, students, and staff in celebration of shared
traditions and the enduring Hwa Chong spirit.</p>
