<p><strong><u>Book Wizards 2025</u></strong>
</p>
<p>On 25 January 2025, Hwa Chong Institution reaffirmed its commitment to
its partnership with the Singapore Indian Development Association (SINDA)
at the opening ceremony of <em>Book Wizards 2025</em>. Looking ahead, Hwa
Chong aims to expand its contributions through increased volunteer participation
and deeper involvement in the programme’s planning. The school also hopes
to broaden its collaboration with SINDA to support more initiatives that
give back to the community.</p>

<p>Launched in June 2024, the <em>Book Wizards</em> programme focuses on developing
phonological awareness and literacy skills through experiential learning
while fostering social-emotional growth in both volunteers and beneficiaries.
Over 20 weeks, 25 Hwa Chong students mentored children from underprivileged
backgrounds, using the <em>Timmy and Tammy</em> book series to teach English.
Their efforts extended beyond traditional learning sessions, as they accompanied
the children on experiential learning journeys to places featured in the
books, such as the Night Safari and Bird Paradise. This mix of online and
in-person interactions not only enriched the children’s learning but also
allowed Hwa Chong students to form meaningful connections with their <em>Book Wizards</em> students
and their families.</p>
