<p>We are also proud to celebrate our students who have been selected to
represent Singapore on the international stage.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Ang Khai Yoong (25S6A) will represent Singapore at the Asian Physics Olympiad
(APhO) in South Korea from 17 to 25 May 2026 while Ng Chern Yi (25S6H)
will represent Singapore at the International Physics Olympiad (IPhO) in
Colombia from 4 to 12 July 2026. Jethro Chai Tsin Kai (25S6H) and Yuan
Yixiang (25S6G) have also been selected to represent Singapore at the International
Nuclear Science Olympiad (INSO) from 2 to 9 August 2026.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p></p>
<p>A heartfelt thank you to our Olympiad coordinators, trainers, seniors,
and school leaders for their unwavering guidance and support. Congratulations
once again to all our students, and we wish our national representatives
every success in the competitions ahead.</p>
