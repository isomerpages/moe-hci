<p>On 16 Jan 2026, Hwa Chong Institution (College Section) opened its doors
to nearly 2800 guests, including prospective students, parents, alumni,
and community members. This year’s Open House showcased the vibrancy of
Hwa Chong’s school culture through the colourful school spirit, extensive
co-curricular activities, and diverse academic pathways that shape the
Hwa Chong Experience.</p>

<p>Throughout the action-packed afternoon, guests caught a glimpse of the
Hwa Chong Experience as they engaged in the various activities located
across the College Section. From the multitude of CCAs located in the Indoor
Sports Hall, College Hall and the Wings of the Central Plaza, to the Key
Events Showcase held in our state-of-the-art classrooms, visitors explored
the wide range of opportunities that awaited them in Hwa Chong.</p>

<p>Concurrently, the Library showcased the diverse academic pathways that
Hwa Chong offers through the various subject and Special Programme booths
hosted by students and staff. Talks hosted in our Lecture Theatres also
provided greater insight on the Hwa Chong Experience.</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/News and Events/College Open House 2026/4.jpg">

<p>In the heart of the College Section, the Central Plaza, CCAs put up performances
in a display of our lively and diverse school culture. The CCA Showcases
also featured performances and Faculty cheers by all four Faculty Committees,
followed by a mass SODACHE (Song, Dance and Cheer) session featuring classic
College Songs. The proceedings of Open House 2026 ended with “Rock You”,
an iconic College Cheer, performed in the Central Plaza by Student Councillors,
Faculty Committees, CCA Leaders and other members of the school community
in display of the #4Facs1Hwach (4 Faculties, 1 Hwa Chong) Spirit.</p>
