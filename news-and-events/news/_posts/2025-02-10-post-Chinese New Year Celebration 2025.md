<p>Kicking off the Chinese New Year festivities, the school gathered for
a joyous combined assembly at the terraces, where our Principal Mr. Lee
Peck Ping shared his well wishes, a spectacular dragon dance performance
thrilled the audience, and Cai Shen made a special visit to spread prosperity
and good fortune.</p>

<p>The High School celebrations began with a lively lion dance performance
by our Scouts, setting a festive tone for the day! The crowd was thrilled
by vibrant group performances, including a medley of joyous tunes by the
Chinese Orchestra and School Band. Adding a unique twist, our talented
teachers delivered a lively song performance, while students amazed the
audience with breakdancing and wushu moves, creating a high-energy showcase
that left everyone in awe.</p>

<p>The spotlight also shone on individual talents, such as an engaging crosstalk
performance by Wang Haoyu, Lucas (1P2), and Wayne Du Yihui (1P2), which
brought laughter and smiles all around. Fwa Shao Xuan Lyon (3O1) mesmerised
the audience with his guzheng solo, while Mr Yeong Chan Kong from the MTL
department captivated everyone with fun and fascinating insights about
the Year of the Snake. The celebration continued with a dynamic wushu performance
before ending on a heartwarming note with a whole-school singalong session
led by the Chinese teachers.</p>

<p>The College Concert was a dazzling showcase of talent, bringing together
diverse CCA groups for a morning of unforgettable performances. The Chinese
Orchestra and Weyoung set the stage with enchanting melodies, filling the
air with festive cheer. The Wushu team stunned the audience with their
precise, powerful movements, while the PSG mesmerised with a dynamic dragon
dance. MAD Chinese performers captivated with a beautifully rich cultural
display, adding depth and tradition to the celebration. An interactive
gameshow injected excitement, as students eagerly joined in, their enthusiasm
matched by the crowd’s roaring cheers. To top it all off, the Students’
Council (SECCo) ignited the festive spirit with a high-energy Chinese New
Year sing-along, leaving everyone on a high note.</p>
