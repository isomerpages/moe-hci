<p>On 2 August, our High School and College students took part in the "Xin
Kong Xia"《新空下》 National Schools Xinyao Singing and Songwriting Competition
2025 finals held at Dunman High School.</p>

talent, creativity, and teamwork, culminating in outstanding results.</p>

<p>Hwa Chong Institution also clinched 1st place in the Songwriting (Creative
Category) for the third consecutive year, a testament to our students’
creativity and passion for music. We look forward to the release of the
music video for《小鱼》 by Zhao Deliang (25S69) and Xu Le (25S69) in 2026.</p>
<p>This year’s results once again highlight how our students rose above challenges.
From countless rehearsals to balancing rigorous academics with intensive
practice, they displayed determination, discipline, and teamwork. Many
students stepped outside their comfort zones to perform on stage for the
first time and poured personal stories into original compositions, striving
for excellence.</p>
<p>We are proud of everyone’s efforts and commend the dedication and passion
of our students!</p>
